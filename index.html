Sustainable Living Pods

Abstract

Sustainable Living Pods is a future systems project that engages with environmental consciousness at a community level, using the emerging technology of 3D printing in order to create reusable planters.

Introduction

My future system, Sustainable Living Pods, utilizes 3D printing in order to create reusable planters as a system that supports a future of sustainable lifestyles.

Methodology
